Borough News

ICE Detention Center

The planned ICE Detention Center is located in Tremont Township NOT Tremont Borough. All questions, concerns, interview requests, R-T-K request, etc., should be directed to the Tremont Township Supervisors. The Tremont Township Meetings are held the 1st Monday of every month at 166 Molleystown Road, Pine Grove PA 17963.

Ashes & Yard Waste

The Borough will pick up ashes and bagged leaves / yard waste every Monday morning starting April 20, 2026.

About Tremont Borough

Nestled in the scenic landscapes of Schuylkill County, Tremont is a historic borough with a rich heritage, offering a charming small-town atmosphere and serving as a reminder of Pennsylvania's unique history.
