ICE DETENTION CENTER

The planned ICE Detention Center is located in Tremont Township NOT Tremont Borough. All questions, concerns, interview requests, R-T-K request, etc., should be directed to the Tremont Township Supervisors. The Tremont Township Meetings are held the 1st Monday of every month at 166 Molleystown Road, Pine Grove PA 17963.

About Us

Tremont, Pennsylvania, nestled in the scenic landscapes of Schuylkill County, is a historic borough with a rich heritage. Located in the northeastern part of the state, Tremont offers a charming small-town atmosphere and serves as a reminder of Pennsylvania's unique history.
