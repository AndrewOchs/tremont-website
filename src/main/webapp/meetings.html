Meetings

Council Meetings

  • Workshop Meetings are held the 1st Tuesday of every month
  • Regular Council Meetings are held the 2nd Tuesday of every month
  • All meetings begin at 6:30 p.m. and are open to the public
  • Tremont Borough Building, 139 Clay Street, Tremont
  • Special meetings and/or workshops will be duly posted and/or advertised
